Unified Accessibility.

At Unified Accessibility (UACC.AI), we envision a world where digital products and services are accessible to everyone. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to create products and services that embrace diversity and inclusivity. With AI at the heart of our solutions and human understanding at the core, we are building the next-generation accessibility platform and expertise to empower organizations to design and build products that delight one and all.

Our Approach

At Unified Accessibility (UACC.AI), we believe that true innovation comes from the combination of AI and Human Intelligence (HI). While AI delivers speed, precision, and scalability, HI—the wisdom, experience, and empathy of people—adds depth and understanding. Together, they form a powerful force that drives our mission to build inclusive, accessible products and services.

Our Impact

At Unified Accessibility (UACC.AI), we transform how organizations approach accessibility, empowering them to create digital products and services that truly serve everyone. By combining the power of AI with the empathy and insight of Human Intelligence (HI), we help businesses deliver inclusive experiences that are not only compliant with accessibility standards but also designed to delight and engage all users.

Product Development Features

Customer Experience Journey Evaluation

AI-powered insights for Product Owners to analyze and refine customer journeys, ensuring they are accessible and inclusive for all users.

Design Usability & Simplicity Insights

AI-powered insights for Designers to evaluate the usability and accessibility of designs.

Accessible Code Development

Comprehensive resources for Engineers on writing accessible code, following global standards such as WCAG to ensure digital assets are compliant.

Automated Accessibility Testing

Quality Assurance Engineers can leverage automated tools to test websites, ensuring accessibility compliance.

Organizational Functional Features

Risk & Compliance Management

Tools for Risk and Compliance Teams to track, manage, and report on accessibility conformance risks, helping meet legal and organizational standards while mitigating risks.

Accessible Procurement Processes

Procurement Teams can access guidance on procuring accessible products and services that meet both organizational goals and accessibility regulations.

Workplace Accommodation Support

Features for HR Teams to handle reasonable accommodation requests, ensuring that workplaces are inclusive, and support for employees with disabilities is comprehensive and well-managed.

Inclusive Talent Acquisition

Tools for Talent Acquisition Leaders to create accessible job descriptions and recruitment platforms, ensuring everyone, regardless of ability, can apply and participate in the hiring process.

IT Onboarding & Desktop Support

Resources for IT Departments on how to onboard employees with disabilities and provide accessible desktop support, ensuring seamless integration into the workplace.
